Why menopause needs to be talked about

For many women, menopause arrives quietly — without preparation, without explanation, and without support.

Menopause is not a single moment or a simple biological change. It is a long and deeply personal transition that can affect the body, emotions, relationships, confidence, and daily life.

Changes in mood, sleep, memory, energy, or physical comfort are often dismissed as stress, personality changes, or “just part of getting older.” When these experiences are misunderstood or ignored, women are left feeling unseen and doubting themselves.

The lack of open conversation means many women go through this phase silently — managing discomfort, confusion, and emotional shifts on their own.

Haven&Her exists to gently break that silence. To help women understand what is happening to them, and to help families support them with patience, clarity, and care.
