The problem we are solving

Menstrual health issues are rarely addressed early. Across families, symptoms are ignored, normalized, or dismissed as stress, age, or personality changes.

From teenage years to adulthood and menopause, women are often told to “wait it out” — until the problem becomes serious enough to require medical intervention.

Why this problem is ignored

Menstrual health is still treated as a private, uncomfortable topic inside families. Conversations stop at periods — not at pain, mood changes, fatigue, or long-term hormonal effects.

As a result, families don’t know what signs matter, when to act, or how to support without overreacting.

Our solution

Haven&Her is a menstrual health companion designed for three life stages — teenage, adult, and menopausal — with two clear perspectives: For Me and To Support Someone.

Instead of overwhelming users with data, the platform focuses on noticing patterns, identifying early warning signs, and encouraging timely care — before problems escalate.

Why Haven&Her is different

Most health apps focus on tracking or diagnosis. Haven&Her focuses on prevention and understanding.

It brings menstrual health into family awareness — helping daughters, partners, and caregivers respond with empathy and clarity, instead of confusion or silence.

By encouraging early attention, Haven&Her aims to reduce delayed diagnosis, untreated hormonal disorders, and long-term health complications.
